FAQ

Got questions?

We've got answers.

Do I need technical skills to set this up?

Not at all. Schedulr is designed to be plug-and-play. Most users are up and running in under 10 minutes with zero coding required.

Can I review and approve AI responses before they're sent?

Absolutely. That's a core feature. The AI drafts responses and suggests bookings — but nothing is sent until you approve it. You remain in full control.

Will this scale as my business grows?

Yes. Our Pro and Business plans are built to handle thousands of conversations daily. The cloud infrastructure scales automatically with your demand.

What platforms does Schedulr integrate with?

We integrate with major booking platforms, CRMs, and messaging channels. Custom integrations are available on the Business plan.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! Every plan comes with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to get started.
